EDA learning summary

for this assignment, I performed Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) 
on the MBA Supply Chain dataset. The dataset contained 
information divided into various columns and each affect the outcome.

During the analysis, I learned how to load a dataset using pandas 
and use various library functions like head,info and describle.

I also checked for blank spaces and duplicate data to understand and 
improve the quality of data.

After that i used seaborn and matplotlib to visualise the data in various forms.

This assignment helped me gain practical experience with python
libraries which were pandas,numpy,seaborn and matplotlob.
